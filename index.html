Farmer Advisory Platform

Empowering rural agricultural communities with data-backed recommendations, weather risk alerts, and harvest optimization across 30+ regions.

  • Problem: Smallholder farmers lacked real-time localized analytics, causing low yield, overuse of inputs, and income variability.
  • Approach: Built an end-to-end solution with data ingestion (satellite weather, soil sensors), automated forecast engine, and decision support dashboards for mobile and desk users.
  • Tech Stack: Python, FastAPI, React, PostgreSQL, Power BI, Azure Functions, Docker, Celery, Redis.
  • Result: 19% measured yield gain, 34% reduction in nitrogen waste, 12% revenue lift per farm, and 92% weekly active user retention (4,200+ farmers).
